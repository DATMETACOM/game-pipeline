## 🎯 CRITERIA FOR HIGH PROFIT + LOW CPI

### CPI Success Factors:
1. **3-second readability** - User understands instantly
2. **Mass appeal** - Broad demographic appeal
3. **Emotional hook** - Curiosity or satisfaction
4. **Ad-friendly** - Works in video ads
5. **Searchability** - Easy to remember & spell
6. **Differentiation** - Stands out in crowded market
7. **Family-friendly** - No red flags for app stores

### Monetization Success Factors:
1. **Premium feel** - Sounds like quality game
2. **Clear promise** - Implies satisfying gameplay
3. **Addictive implication** - "One more level" feeling
4. **Payoff telegraphing** - Name promises reward
5. **Shareable** - Fun to say/show friends
